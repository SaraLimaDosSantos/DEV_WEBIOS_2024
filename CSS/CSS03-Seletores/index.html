The first department store was created in Paris, in 1850. It was conceived as a big place with a lot of options in products and brands. They sell just a specific product: there you could find clothes, shoes, jewels, make up and many others things. That concept remained until today. So, if you want to buy many things with good prices, you can choose from one of the thousands in the world.
